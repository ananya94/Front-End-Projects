Classes

Coding Temple is an accelerated learning computer programming bootcamp located in Chicago. Our 10-week curriculum is designed to prepare students for a successful career in software and web development. We created our company in 2015 with a group of 5 leaders and a goal to teach beginners the in’s and out’s of the coding world. Coding Temple offers both full and part-time courses in Chicago. Currently Coding Temple offers Full Stack .NET, Python, and JavaScript courses. Coding Temple is expanding to Washington DC Spring of 2017!
